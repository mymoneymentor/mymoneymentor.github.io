ICICI Bank Credit Card Eligibility

  • Minimum age of 21.
  • ICICI bank salaried customer – minimum annual income required 2.40 lac per annum.
  • Non- ICICI bank salaried customer - minimum annual income required 3.60 lac per annum.
  • Self employed and holding savings accounts with ICICI bank- minimum ITR required 3.60 lac.
  • Self employed and non holding savings accounts with ICICI bank – minimum ITR required 4.80 lac

How to apply for credit card online

    The ICICI Bank Credit Card application process is very easy. Here are the steps to apply for a Credit Card:
  • Click on Apply Now
  • Select your Credit Card as per preference and check eligibility.
  • Validate your address and employment information.
  • Complete the authorisation process with either NetBanking or a Debit card.
  • After an internal review, we will provide you with a new Credit Card.
